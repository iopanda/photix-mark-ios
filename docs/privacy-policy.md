# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 6, 2026**

PhotixMark ("the App") is developed by iopanda. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Summary

**PhotixMark does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.** All processing happens entirely on your device.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- Photos or image files
- EXIF metadata (camera make, model, lens, location, date, etc.)
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Any personally identifiable information

## How the App Works

All photo processing — including EXIF extraction, watermark rendering, and file export — is performed **locally on your device**. No photo, metadata, or any other data is ever sent to external servers or third parties.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or cloud services.

## Permissions

The App may request access to your Photo Library solely to allow you to import and export photos. This access is used only within the App and is never transmitted externally.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will be available at this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
