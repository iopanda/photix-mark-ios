## Description
> Max 4,000 characters.

```
PhotixMark lets you add professional EXIF watermarks to your photos in just a few taps — no subscription, no cloud upload, no compromise on privacy.

Simply import your photos and PhotixMark automatically reads the camera metadata embedded in each file: brand, model, lens, focal length, aperture, shutter speed, ISO, and date. Pick a template, tweak the style, and export — your photos never leave your device.

KEY FEATURES

• Auto EXIF Reading
  Camera make, model, lens, focal length, aperture, shutter speed, ISO, and date/time are extracted automatically from every photo.

• 10 Curated Templates
  From clean minimal captions to bold brand marks and blurred-background layouts — find the style that fits your vision.

• Batch Processing
  Apply a template to all photos or a hand-picked selection at once. Process dozens of photos in seconds.

• Custom Brand Logos
  Upload your own logo for any camera brand. PhotixMark will display it automatically whenever it detects that brand in your photos.

• EXIF Editor
  Need to correct a field? Manually override any metadata — make, model, lens, aperture, date — before rendering.

• Fine-Grained Template Settings
  Control which EXIF fields are shown, adjust text and background colors, toggle the logo, enable drop shadows, and tune padding — per photo if you like.

• 100% On-Device
  Zero network requests. Your photos and metadata stay on your device at all times.

• Multilingual
  Fully localized in English, 简体中文, 繁體中文, 日本語, and 한국어. Follows your system language automatically.

PERFECT FOR
  — Photography enthusiasts who share work on social media
  — Camera reviewers and gear bloggers
  — Anyone who wants to brand their photos with shooting parameters

PhotixMark is inspired by the open-source web project photix-mark-web by LeoonLiang. The iOS & macOS app brings the same concept natively to your devices.
```
